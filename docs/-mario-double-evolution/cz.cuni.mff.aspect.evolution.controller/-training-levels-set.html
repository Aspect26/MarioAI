TrainingLevelsSet

val TrainingLevelsSet: Array<MarioLevel>

Super Mario levels data set. Contains original Super Mario Bros game levels Stage 1 Level 1, Stage 2 Level 1, Stage 4 Level 1 and Stage 5 Level 1 split into smaller parts, as well as one flat level (doesn't contain any obstacles and is flat), and one level containing only holes.
